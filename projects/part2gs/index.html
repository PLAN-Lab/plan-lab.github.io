Part²GS: Part-aware Modeling of articulated Objects using 3D Gaussian Splatting

Tianjiao Yu, Vedant Shah, Muntasir Wahed, Shen Ying, Kiet A. Nguyen, Ismini Lourentzou
University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign
Paper arXiv Code (coming soon)
TL;DR: We propose Part²GS, a part-aware framework for modeling articulated objects using 3D Gaussian Splatting. Our method introduces motion-informed part discovery and physically grounded articulation learning, guided by contact, velocity, and vector-field constraints. Part²GS outperforms state-of-the-art baselines on geometry and motion fidelity, enabling clean part disentanglement and collision-free articulation in complex multi-part objects.
